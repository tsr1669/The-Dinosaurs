Allosaurus is a genus of large carnosaurian theropod dinosaur that lived 155 to 145 million years ago during the Late Jurassic epoch. The name "Allosaurus" means "different lizard" alluding to its unique concave vertebrae. It is derived from the Greek ἄλλος and σαῦρος. 

       
        Speed: 30 – 55 km/h
        Lived: 163.5 million years ago - 89.3 million years ago (Late Jurassic - Turonian)
        Eats: Stegosaurus, Brachiosauruss
